# AI_ML_CLASS_coupons

How does Income Level, Educaation, and Marital Status Effect the Acceptance of Coupons for Cheap Restaurants and More Expensive Restaurants?

Coupons Acceptance Report


One of the most interesting findings was comparing income to the coupon acceptance of cheap restaurants and more expensive restaurants.
The cheap restaurants were accepted more often for all income levels, but one thing that I thought was most interesting was that the higher incomes were not as interested in coupons for restaurants as the mid-range income levels.

![image](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/907bab5f-669f-4204-9134-ef684a274b00)


When looking at marital status, it looked like single people with kids were the most likely to accept coupons.  This could be because it is more difficult financially for people raising kids without a partner.
I then noticed that the single people with kids accepted at a rate of 100% and that made me think that maybe there isn't a lot of data in that category.

![image](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/1a164694-cd23-49d2-a5cc-d5fde0295c75)

I looked further, I realized that there were only 3 people in the dataset who were single and had kids in the car when the coupon was distributed, so this might not be enough data to make a good conclusion.
![image](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/61cf2f03-0ab0-4d52-8f0d-1f049e5956bd)
